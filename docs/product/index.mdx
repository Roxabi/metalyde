## The Marketing Agency OS

Metalyde is the operating layer for how marketing agencies run their business.

Not a project management tool. Not a CRM. Not a PSA retrofitted with agency templates. A purpose-built vertical OS that spans every surface an agency operates on — from the moment a brief comes in to the moment an invoice goes out.

The category exists. No one has claimed it at the agency layer. That is the bet.

## Who it is for

Metalyde is built for the operator running a 2-10 person agency doing $50K–$500K in client revenue.

That person writes briefs, reviews deliverables, chases approvals, invoices clients, and monitors margins — often in the same afternoon. They are not looking for another tool that needs a 90-minute onboarding. They are looking for something that already knows how an agency works.

## The problem

The cost of fragmented operations is documented and specific:

- **$1,000–$5,000 lost per month** to scope creep that never gets billed (57% of agencies).
- **$32,000 lost per year** on average to untracked time.
- **4–6 hours per week** spent bridging two disconnected tools.
- **~60% utilization** against a healthy target of 65–80%.

These are not inefficiency metrics. They are revenue leaving the business every month.

## What Metalyde does

Metalyde covers the six operating surfaces an agency runs on:

| Surface | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **Briefs** | Structured intake that locks scope before work starts. |
| **Delivery** | Track what is in flight, who owns it, and what is blocked. |
| **Billing** | Surface unbilled scope and time before it disappears. |
| **Reporting** | Profitability first — margins per account, not vanity metrics. |
| **AI execution** | Agents that do the operational work, not just suggest it. |
| **Multi-tenancy** | One platform, isolated per agency — secure by default. |

## Status

Metalyde is a proof-of-concept.

The positioning, design system, and multi-tenant infrastructure are production-grade and live. The product features and dashboards are what comes next.

What is already done: category framing, brand voice, design tokens, auth, multi-tenant data isolation (Postgres RLS), CI/CD pipeline, and the full monorepo stack. What is being built: the operating surfaces above.
