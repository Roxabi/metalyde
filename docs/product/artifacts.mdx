## Overview

These artifacts were produced during the positioning track and capture the decisions behind the category bet, the buyer, the voice, the visual direction, and the first public-facing concept. They are living documents — served via Forge and updated as the product and market understanding evolve. Use them as the source of truth for any copy, design, or messaging work on Metalyde.

## Artifacts

| Artifact | What it contains |
|---|---|
| **[Positioning Exploration](https://forge.roxabi.dev/metalyde/metalyde-positioning-exploration)** | Category bets, positioning angles, competitive landscape, differentiators, and anti-positioning. The foundation for every claim we make about what Metalyde is and is not. |
| **[Customer Personas & Voice](https://forge.roxabi.dev/metalyde/metalyde-customer-personas)** | Who the buyer is, how they describe their day, and the exact language they use. The input for all messaging and copy decisions. |
| **[Brand Voice & Messaging Framework](https://forge.roxabi.dev/metalyde/metalyde-messaging-framework)** | The five voice attributes, tone spectrum, writing rules, vocabulary canon, messaging pillars, and tagline candidates. The reference for anyone writing anything under the Metalyde brand. |
| **[Visual Direction Exploration](https://forge.roxabi.dev/metalyde/metalyde-visual-directions)** | Visual direction candidates and design-system rationale. Input for the UI and design token decisions already implemented in the stack. |
| **[Landing Page PoC](https://forge.roxabi.dev/metalyde/metalyde-landing-poc)** | The live landing concept built around "The OS for a marketing agency" — the first expression of the positioning in product form. |
